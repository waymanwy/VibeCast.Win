About the Replace / Enter toggles
  • These live on the phone page itself, not here — they're a per-visit choice, not a PC-wide setting.
  • Replace off (default) inserts at the cursor; on, it clears the field first.
  • Enter presses Enter right after sending — handy for chat boxes, off by default.
