Not working?
  • Tap the microphone icon on your own keyboard (Gboard etc.) to dictate — VibeCast just relays the text.
  • On the PC, click the input box you want the text to land in before pressing Send.
  • "Replace" clears the field first instead of inserting, for overwriting a search box or address bar instead of inserting at the cursor.
  • "Send to PC" just types the text; "Send + Enter" also presses Enter right after.
  • Opened without a code? Scan the QR code from the PC tray icon again.
